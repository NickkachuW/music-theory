# Daily Practice Routine - Quick Reference Card
## Latin Jazz & Tango Saxophone Improvisation

---

## 10-MINUTE WARM-UP (Every Day)
1. Long tones on the tonic of today's key (2 min)
2. Major scale ascending/descending, full range (2 min)
3. Dorian scale in today's key with clave foot tap (3 min)
4. Minor pentatonic in today's key, varying rhythms (3 min)

---

## PHASE 1 ROUTINE (Weeks 1-4) - 30 min total

| Block | Time | Activity |
|-------|------|----------|
| Scales | 10 min | Dorian + Mixolydian in 2 keys, with clave |
| Patterns | 10 min | Minor pentatonic + Blues scale in same keys |
| Improvise | 10 min | Play over a latin jazz backing track (Dm7-G7 vamp) |

### Key Rotation:
- Mon: D Dorian / G Mixolydian
- Tue: G Dorian / C Mixolydian
- Wed: A Dorian / D Mixolydian
- Thu: E Dorian / A Mixolydian
- Fri: C Dorian / F Mixolydian
- Sat: F Dorian / Bb Mixolydian
- Sun: Review weakest keys + free improvisation

---

## PHASE 2 ROUTINE (Weeks 5-8) - 35 min total

| Block | Time | Activity |
|-------|------|----------|
| New scales | 10 min | Spanish/Jewish + Harmonic Minor in 2 keys |
| ii-V-i | 10 min | Dorian -> Spanish -> Harm. Minor, cycle 3 keys |
| Bebop lines | 5 min | Bebop Dominant scale, continuous 8th notes |
| Improvise | 10 min | Latin ii-V-i backing track, use all scales |

### Key Rotation for ii-V-i:
- Mon: C minor (Dm-G7b9-Cm)
- Tue: G minor (Am-D7b9-Gm)
- Wed: D minor (Em-A7b9-Dm)
- Thu: F minor (Gm-C7b9-Fm)
- Fri: A minor (Bm-E7b9-Am)
- Sat: Bb minor (Cm-F7b9-Bbm)
- Sun: Cycle all 6 keys back to back

---

## PHASE 3 ROUTINE (Weeks 9-12) - 40 min total

| Block | Time | Activity |
|-------|------|----------|
| Altered scale | 10 min | Dim. Whole Tone in 2 keys + resolve to Dorian |
| Tension comparison | 10 min | Same V7: Mixolydian vs Spanish vs Altered vs Whole Tone |
| Phrygian vamps | 5 min | Phrygian over dark modal grooves |
| Transcription | 5 min | Learn 4 bars of a Paquito/Sanchez/Barbieri solo |
| Improvise | 10 min | Full latin tune, mixing all tension levels |

---

## PHASE 4 ROUTINE (Weeks 13-16) - 45 min total

| Block | Time | Activity |
|-------|------|----------|
| Tango cadences | 10 min | Phrygian cadence (bII-V7b9-i) in 3 keys |
| Advanced scales | 10 min | Lydian Aug / Locrian #2 / 6th Mode Harm Min |
| Tango vocabulary | 10 min | Chromatic neighbors, dramatic leaps, 3-3-2 rhythm |
| Transcription | 5 min | Piazzolla melody or Gato Barbieri lick |
| Full improv | 10 min | Alternate between latin jazz and tango feels |

---

## SCALE CHEAT SHEET: What to Play Over What

```
Major 7     ->  Major, Lydian
Dominant 7  ->  Mixolydian, Bebop Dominant
Dom 7b9     ->  Spanish/Jewish, Diminished (H-W)
Dom 7alt    ->  Altered (Dim Whole Tone)
Dom 7#5     ->  Whole Tone
Dom 7sus4   ->  Mixolydian (avoid 3rd), Pentatonic on b7
Minor 7     ->  Dorian, Minor Pentatonic, Blues
Min-Maj 7   ->  Melodic Minor, Harmonic Minor
Half-dim    ->  Locrian #2
Dim 7       ->  Diminished (W-H)
Modal vamp  ->  Phrygian (dark), Dorian (bright)
```

## LISTENING HOMEWORK

### Latin Jazz (prioritize these):
- **Paquito D'Rivera** - any album (alto sax model)
- **David Sanchez** - "Obsesion" (tenor sax model)
- **Jerry Gonzalez & Fort Apache Band** - "Rumba Para Monk"
- **Chucho Valdes** - any album (piano, but great for understanding harmony)
- **Eddie Palmieri** - "Sueno" (montuno/vamp mastery)
- **Mongo Santamaria** - "Afro Blue" (rhythmic feel)
- **Michel Camilo** - "One More Once" (modern latin jazz)

### Tango (prioritize these):
- **Gato Barbieri** - "Chapter One: Latin America" (tenor sax + tango)
- **Astor Piazzolla** - "Tango: Zero Hour" (the tango bible)
- **Javier Girotto** - "Aires Tangueros" (sax + modern tango)
- **Piazzolla** - "Libertango", "Oblivion", "Adios Nonino" (transcribe these)
- **Dino Saluzzi** - "Cite de la Musique" (bandoneon, atmosphere)

### Transcription Priority:
1. Week 1-4: Simple pentatonic-based latin jazz solos
2. Week 5-8: Solos with clear ii-V-i movement
3. Week 9-12: Outside playing, tension/resolution solos
4. Week 13-16: Piazzolla melodies, Gato Barbieri solos
