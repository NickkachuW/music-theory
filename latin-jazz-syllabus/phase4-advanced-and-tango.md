### Tango Melodic Vocabulary Exercises

**Exercise 1: Chromatic Neighbor Tones**
In each key, play the harmonic minor scale but approach each note from a half step below:
- B-C, C#-D, D#-Eb, E#-F, F#-G, G#-Ab, A#-B (in C harmonic minor)

**Exercise 2: Dramatic Leaps + Stepwise Resolution**
- Jump up a 6th or 7th, then walk back down stepwise
- This is a signature tango melodic gesture

**Exercise 3: 3-3-2 Rhythmic Grouping**
Play any scale with this accent pattern over 4/4:
```
Accent: X . . X . . X .
Beats:  1 & 2 & 3 & 4 &
```
This is the tango rhythmic DNA - related to the habanera rhythm.

**Exercise 4: Marcato vs Sincopa**
- Marcato: Play quarter notes, heavy and accented (like walking)
- Sincopa: Play with syncopation, pushing ahead of the beat
- Practice switching between the two in the middle of a phrase
